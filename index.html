About

"Travelease" can refer to a couple of different entities, including the Travel Ease full-service travel agency established in 1987 that plans vacation packages and handles airfare and logistics, and TravelEase.com, an online travel planning and booking platform for vacations, hotels, flights, car rentals, and local activities. There are also other companies with similar names, such as TravelEase India Private Limited and the GM modular TraveEase Pro headphones and chargers. Travel Ease is a licensed full service Travel Agency, appointed by Airline Reporting Corporation (ARC) and International Airlines Transport Association (IATA). We are also members with good standing with the American Society of Travel Agents (ASTA) and TravelSavers. This agency has been in business since 1987 and has a combined experience of 110 years in the travel industry. The continuing hallmark of our success is our integrity which is the foundation for our team of conscientious professionals. Our ability to deliver on our commitments reflects our paradigm of excellent customer service. Travel Ease has become a pioneer in the travel industry. Our growth in an industry so competitive was not without assistance. We had help - from our clients.
